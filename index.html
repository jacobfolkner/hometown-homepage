Things to do while in Alexandria

Alexandria Theater

Come see a movie at the local theater and bring your own bowl to get as much popcorn as you want!

Alexandria Library

Come to the historic Carnegie Library and check out some town history, join a group activity, or just relax and read a book.

Largest Ball of Paint

Come see the largest ball of paint in the world! It's right here in Alexandria only 1 mile from my house, and if you call ahead he will even let you add a layer of paint to the ball yourself!
